Products

FaceQuest ® 1:1 Facial Recognition Technology

FaceQuest’s 1:1 Facial Recognition Technology answers the question -- "Do these two photos belong to the same person?". The user provides two pictures and the software tells if they belong to the same person or not.

Applications

  • Paperless KYC
  • ATM Fraud Prevention
  • Smart Doors
  • Paperless check-in for Airlines

FaceQuest ® 1:N Facial Recognition Technology

FaceQuest’s 1:N Facial Recognition Technology answers the question -- "Who is the person in this photograph?". This product searches the known-faces database and finds out if the given image contains any known people.

Applications

  • Customer Loyalty Improvement
  • Visitor Management
  • Contactless Attendance
  • Smart Apartments
