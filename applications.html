Industrial Uses of FaceQuest ®

Secure ATM Transactions

  • Banking
FaceQuest’s face recognition service can be used to secure ATM transactions. When a user tries to access the ATM machine by inserting the debit card, the camera on the kiosk will capture the image of the user & search it through the database of the account holder and match it with the photograph on his account. The user will still login with his PIN, but in case of the photograph not matching the profile an otp will be sent to the account holder via sms/email so that they can recognise or report the transaction. If an account holder wants to authorise multiple users to use his debit card, he can request it to the bank at anytime & update them with the photographs of the additional users.
  • Additional layer of security
  • Prevent frauds

Paperless KYC

  • E-Wallets
  • Ride Sharing
FaceQuest’s 1:1 Face Verification can be used to verify the photo of a person with the photo on the ID. Collect a selfie from the partner or customer and easily match the face with the one present in their ID. Save significant amount on manual verification procedures.

Prevent Insurance Fraud

  • Insurance
FaceQuest can helps banks or life insurance companies, in order to prevent fraudulent claims. While buying a life insurance policy, the insured person would need to submit a photograph of the nominee(s) and ever year an updated photograph of the nominee(s) can be submitted by the insured person to the bank or life insurance company. In case of the death of the insured person & the nominee(s) requesting claim of amount for the life insurance policy, the insurance navigator click a photograph of the nominee(s) & run it through the FaceQuest solution in order to match the photograph with the photograph submitted by the insured person earlier. In case there is not a significant match of the 2 photographs, the investigator can take an action to scrutinise the investigation.

Increase Customer Loyalty

  • Banking
  • Retail
  • Hospitality
FaceQuest can be integrated with motion detector cameras installed at the entry point of the bank. As soon as a customer walks in, the camera will click an image & run it through database of premium customers. In case FaceQuest matches a customer entering with a premium customer, this can be notified to the relationship manager, and the customer can be given priority service.

Visitor Management

  • Workplaces
FaceQuest can be integrated with motion detector cameras installed at the entry point of the office/building. As soon as a visitor walks in, the camera will click an image & run it through database of past visitors. In case FaceQuest matches visitor in the past visitor data, this can be notified to security or front desk executive to streamline seamless entry. Visitor maybe a customer, contractor, consultant, job applicant or a delivery person.

Manage Staff Attendance

  • Workplaces
FaceQuest’s face recognition service can also be used for taking staff attendance at workplaces. This can be replaced by the old biometric or id scan method of taking attendance. With the help of motion cameras at the workplace entry, an photo can be clicked of a person entering the premises, FaceQuest will then run the photo with the employee database and mark the employee as present. This can also be integrated with cameras inside the office premises or an employee can also get a photograph click on his workstation.

Smart Apartments

  • Real-estate
FaceQuest can be integrated with motion detector cameras installed at the entry point of the apartment buildings or society gates. As soon as a visitor walks in, the camera will click an image & run it through database of past visitors. In case FaceQuest matches visitor in the past visitor data, this can be notified to owner to streamline the approval/denial of entry. Visitor maybe a friend, relative, delivery person, contractor, etc. In case a person who has entry has been banned by the owner, tries to enter, can be notified to local security.

Unlock Doors Smartly

  • Real-estate
FaceQuest’s face recognition service can be integrated with the camera at the entry of an apartment or house in order to unlock the door. Multiple users can be setup by the owner who can unlock the door by their face. Whenever someone comes at the door, the camera will detect a motion and click a photograph of the person standing at the door and run it through the database. If the person matches the door would be unlocked. This can be integrated with a 2 factor authentication of face match & pin to unlock the door.

Streamline Check-in and Boarding Process

  • Airlines
FaceQuest’s facial recognition service can help airlines streamline the check-in & boarding process for their passengers. While booking the flight online, the passenger can upload his image or can upload a photo id proof. The passenger can then directly go to the airline counter to check-in the baggage, the passenger need not print any boarding pass or baggage tag. When a passenger goes to the airline counter, the camera there will click the photograph & run it through the database & streamline the baggage drop process. This will over all reduce the wait time for all the passengers. Also when boarding the flight through the gate, another camera can be placed which will eliminate the usage of scanning the boarding pass & save time for the passengers as well as the airline.

Record Student Attendance

  • Educational Institutions
FaceQuest’s face recognition service can also be used for taking attendance for students in classrooms, labs & even examination rooms. Photograph of the class can be clicked at specific intervals from multiple angles, and then with the student database, the faces can be matched and mark the students as present. This will help the educational institution to reduce manual work and even reduce the total time taken for attendance marking. It will help students to fully utilise their attention towards the class.

Prevent Fraud in Competitive Examinations

  • Educational Institutions
FaceQuest’s face recognition service can be used by competitive testing agencies in order to prevent fraudulent impersonations of test takers. There are various number of cases, where some person impersonates the test taker in order to help him qualify for the test. Testing agencies can click photograph of every test taker appearing for the test, it will run through the test taker database & find for matches, incase it doesn’t find any match, it will store as a new reference face on the database on that uuid of that test taker.
  • To prevent fraud: In case FaceQuest finds a match with a previous test taker, it will be notified to the examination convener or any other concerned person, they can then match the uuid of the new test taker with the old test taker, in case of any discrepancy, the convener can make a decision on whether to allow or disallow the test taker to appear for the test
  • To mark attendance

Increase Safety in Ride & Delivery Services

  • Transport
  • Delivery
Hyperlocal delivery & ride service hailing aggregators mostly rely on delivery or driver partners in order to provide service to the end user. In order to ensure the safety of the end user, the aggregators can ask the partner to click a live photograph from their partner app & submit it to the company database. FaceQuest will help the companies to match the submitted photograph with the photograph stored in the database. It will help the aggregators to identify if some other person is using the partner’s app to provide the service, the aggregator companies can then make a decision to temporarily block the profile of the partner.
