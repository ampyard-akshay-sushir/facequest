FaceQuest ® 1:N Facial Recognition Technology

FaceQuest’s 1:N Facial Recognition Technology answers the question -- "Who is the person in this photograph?". This product searches the known-faces database and finds out if the given image contains any known people.

FaceQuest® 1:N Facial Recognition Technology is a combination of Face Detection & Facial Recognition Search algorithms powered by AI which searches the image of a person from a pool of database & gives information about the person. The reverse image search facial recognition query is named as a Quest.

Useful to

  • Retail organizations for customer loyalty improvement
  • Educational institutions for contactless attendance management
  • Airlines for contactless boarding and check-in
