About FaceQuest

FaceQuest™ is a Face Recognition Platform created and maintained by Ampyard, a private company incorporated in India.

FaceQuest provides advanced, ethical face recognition service to students, developers, and businesses worldwide. Through computer vision and deep learning, we match and recognize faces in photos—our innovative API platform lets developers & businesses add state-of-the-art face recognition capabilities into their own software.

FaceQuest was launched in 2020, with no external investment, completely seed-funded by the co-founders of Ampyard
